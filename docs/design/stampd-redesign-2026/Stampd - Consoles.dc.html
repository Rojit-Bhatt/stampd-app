Stampd · Consoles — desktop

Outlet & platform dashboards

Built on your picks: outlet = OB×OA hybrid (hub dashboard + persistent rail, Earn/Redeem front and centre); platform = PB (top-bar nav) now with a charts dashboard and dashboard-level actions. To stop Stampd reading like a generic RMS, the identity is editorial-ledger: oversized serif numerals as the hero of every metric, hairline "ledger rules" instead of boxed grids, and outlined action cards with a green corner-tab — not a wall of flat filled tiles. The two platform metric families are drawn differently on purpose (see note).

Why this doesn't read like a generic RMS

Three deliberate moves: (1) serif numerals are the loudest thing on screen — data feels authored, not spreadsheet-y; (2) hairline ledger rules separate KPIs instead of a grid of shadowed cards; (3) the two platform metric families are drawn differently on purpose — snapshots are plain bordered cards with no trend, flow metrics get a green left-tab + sparkline + WoW delta — so "how many exist" never gets confused with "how fast it's moving." Charts are Recharts in build; drawn simply here.
