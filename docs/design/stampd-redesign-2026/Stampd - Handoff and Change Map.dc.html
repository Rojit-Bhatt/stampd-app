Stampd · Engineer handoff & change map

What changed, and how to build it

The redesign keeps the Stampd name and original logo but drops the stamp metaphor entirely: points are a numeric, fractional, spendable currency. Backend, data model, routes and business logic are unchanged — this is the visual + interaction layer. Structural changes and any interaction that replaces today's flow are flagged below for your approval before implementation.

01

Design direction

Identity

Stampd — "editorial ledger." Points read like money in a beautifully-kept passbook. Serif numerals (DM Serif Display) are the loudest thing on every screen; hairline rules replace boxed grids; green means value and action. Deliberately not a generic RMS look.

Who / what for

Customer app: mid-range Android first, PWA, floating nav, tenant-themed. Two desktop consoles that feel different on purpose — outlet = fast counter hub, platform = dense dark data desk. Platform identity is fixed and never tenant-themed.

04

Implementation notes

Tokens

Replace legacy warm-cream vars in index.css with the Stampd light+dark sets (Foundations §01). Keep the CSS-var architecture; add a .dark scope.

Tenant color

Keep TenantProvider injecting --tenant; add a contrast helper deriving --tenant-ink/--tenant-on (≥4.5:1). Value/actions read --primary, never --tenant.

Motion & a11y

motion springs per Foundations §05, every animation branched on useReducedMotion. 44px min targets, real focus rings, contrast held.

Primitives

Add deliberately: tabs, dropdown-menu, table, badge, progress, segmented-control, command, select. Charts = Recharts; toasts = react-hot-toast (already in).

Decisions awaiting your sign-off

1) Keep the Stampd name and original logo; drop only the stamp metaphor.   2) The five APPROVE flow changes above.   3) Nav picks confirmed: outlet OB×OA, platform PB.   Everything else is a like-for-like reskin with no behavioural change.
