Questions, answered

Do my customers need to download an app?
No. It runs in the browser and adds to the home screen like an app.
Can I choose my own rewards?
Yes — put a points price on any menu item or a standalone reward, and set how much of a bill comes back.
Is there a payment gateway?
No. Stampd is purely loyalty. Subscriptions are arranged with us directly and activated with a key.
